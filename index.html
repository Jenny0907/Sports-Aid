About Us

Sport Aid is a business that is concerned about the health and well being of sports players. We are a company that hosts an app and Website to provide a service to sports teams all around the world to use. So this app will help coaches and managers to have an easy access to the medical details of their players in case of emergency, and to avoid giving the player medication that they are allergic to. As well as have the players ICE number.

Vision

Our vision is to help lessen the amount of permanent damage caused by time sensitive sports injuries.

Mission

Our mission is to grow the desire for sport by lessening the serious effects of injury.
